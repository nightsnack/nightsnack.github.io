Yuxuan Cai

Yuxuan Cai is a researcher in Foundation Model Group, Megvii Technology, Beijing, China. He graduated from Northeastern University in 2021 with a master degree in Computer Engineering, supervised by Professor Yanzhi Wang. His work has been published in some high-impact conferences (AAAI, NIPS).

His research interests include:

  • Network Architecture Design
  • Foundation Models
  • Self Supervised Learning
  • Model Compression
